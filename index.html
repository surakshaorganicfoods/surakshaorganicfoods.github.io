Who we are?

Suraksha Organic foods is into farming, sourcing, processing and providing quality organic foods to consumers through wholesale and retail and e-commerce avenues. We pride ourselves in living by the motto "By Nature, For Nature. Our aim is to provide to our customers, 'the real organic food, which is produced with the most stringent of checks and procedures to ensure that only the best reaches them. We achieve this by practicing farming techniques devoid of any chemical and/or pesticides, whatsoever. We also empower farmers from whom we source part of our products by way of educational boot camps by working with local governing and scientific bodies regularly.
